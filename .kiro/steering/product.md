# Product Overview

PaperTerrace is an AI-powered research paper reading assistant designed to help English learners and researchers efficiently read academic documents and papers.

## Core Features

- **AI OCR Analysis**: Uses Google Gemini 1.5 Flash to convert complex PDF layouts into readable text while preserving structure
- **Interactive Dictionary**: One-click word lookup with instant Japanese translations and AI-powered explanations for specialized terms
- **AI Reading Assistance**: Multiple AI agents including:
  - Chat assistant for Q&A about paper content
  - Author agent simulation
  - Research radar for finding related papers
  - Smart summarization (full paper and section-wise)
  - Paragraph analysis with background context
  - Figure/chart insights with AI interpretation
  - Adversarial review for critical analysis
- **Modern UI/UX**: Built with FastAPI, React, and Tailwind CSS for smooth, responsive experience

## Target Users

- English language learners reading academic papers
- Researchers needing efficient paper analysis tools
- Students working with complex academic documents

## Key Value Proposition

Reduces the friction of looking up unknown words and understanding complex academic content through AI-powered assistance and contextual explanations.
